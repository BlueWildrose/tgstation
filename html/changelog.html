22 December 2020

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • Adds shrub plants and seeds, which can be obtained from the exotic seeds crate in cargo. Plant grown shrubs in-hand to plant a full hedge where you're standing.
  • The shrub seeds have replaced one of the rainbow bunch seeds in the exotic seeds crate.
  • Hedges that block sight can be trimmed with a sharp implement if you have the hedge trimming skillchip.

Fikou updated:

  • cargo techs now have ripley/clarke mech internal access
  • fixes internal access on clarke not allowing engineers and mining

Ghommie updated:

  • Fixing a little visual issue with certain hoods, fake mustaches and similars making lizard snouts magically disappear when worn.

Rohesie updated:

  • Shades can no longer attack things while inside a soul stone.

That REALLY Good Soda Flavor updated:

  • Reverb while you are affected by a chemical shouldn't cut out anymore.

nightred updated:

  • fixed mob temperature damage when dead

21 December 2020

Ghilker updated:

  • fixed delta station layer manifolds under floor in SM room
  • added some missing lights on delta station SM and atmos
  • added buttons and shutters to the delta station atmos testing room chambers to vent to space

Jared-Fogle updated:

  • You can no longer use communications consoles off a station/CentCom z-level.
  • Modular computers can now be attacked
  • Rerolling blob strains now uses a radial menu, and lets you see what the strain does before picking it.

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Hulks can now throw monkeys/people with tails around by their tails! To do this, get your victim in at least a neck grab, enable throw mode, then click in the direction you want to throw. So long!
  • Carbons will now take damage when thrown at high speeds into things like windows, girders, and other solid objects, in addition to just walls and other people.

spessbro updated:

  • appendices are toxic again

20 December 2020

  • Cyborg Boot Debug now uses tgui. Better check those endoskeletons with a multitool!

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • You can no longer change your body's facing direction while dead or otherwise unconscious.

TemporalOroboros updated:

  • You press your ear to the barrel and hear a faint rumba beat... The blastcannon has been buffed!

Time-Green updated:

  • Fixes locker wand shooting grey lockers

broodmother spider (746) updated:
